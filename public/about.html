ARTIST STATEMENT

I am an immersive installation artist who uses painting, light, and video to create new participatory experiences that incorporate the viewer. I draw on my experience as a scenic artist in theater to achieve puzzling and striking visual effects. In many ways, my installations create a stage for viewers whose movement becomes an essential part of their experience.

My work speaks to the variety of ways a visual event can be experienced. Layers of paint and light build upon each other such that initial layers remain essential to the final image. A variety of gestures make themselves apparent in the work, but it is unclear where one ends and the other starts.

I am interested in how surfaces interact with light and aim to create ambiguity around the materiality of illusions. Thus, I examine the potential of materials to function in diverse ways by experimenting with the often-unanticipated behavior of substances to activate my surfaces.

My experiments with design originate out of my passion for learning new technical and digital skills. I am inspired by the full-scale prototyping that takes place for a new theatrical production, and wonder how this creativity can be applied to other aspects of life.
